## Euler's Formula
$$\begin{align*}
\sin(x) &= \frac{1}{j2}(e^{jx} - e^{-jx}) \\
\cos(x) &= \frac{1}{2}(e^{jx} + e^{-jx}) \\
e^{jx} &= \cos(x) + j\sin(x) \\
e^{-jx} &= \cos(x) - j\sin(x) \\
e^{jx} - e^{-jx} &= j2\sin(x)\\
e^{jx} + e^{-jx} &= 2\cos(x) \\
\end{align*}$$
