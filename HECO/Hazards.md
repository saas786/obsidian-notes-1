Lead:
-	Heavy metal (at room temperature)
-	Corrosion resistant
-	Found in:
	-	batteries
	-	paints and coatings
	-	pipes
	-	metals
	-	ceramics
	-	auto manufacturing
	-	printing
	-	ammunition
	-	gasoline
-	Highest risk of lead exposure
	-	Abrasive blasting
	-	Welding, Cutting, and Burning on steel structures
	-	Grinding
	-	Manual scraping and sanding
	-	Demolition
	-	Spray painting/coating with lead based products
